Ursell Jewelry Art Handmade X Vintage

Ursell Jewelry Art designs and sells jewelry for both women and men of all ages.
Our jewelry and custom design services will be marketed and sold primarily online through
our website at: www.urselljewelryart.com

UJA jewelry will also be available for national and international distribution through
various retail marketplace websites. Specialty stores and boutiques will sell items on
consignment or as contracted locally and regionally. Ursell Jewelry Art Handmade X Vintage
will also seek to sell our jewelry items in certain niche markets such as cruise ships.
